Competitions

  • Kattis (ICPC) -- The International Collegiate Programming Contest is team based competion were a group of three people go and try to complete as many programing based problems as they can.

  • CCDC -- The Colegent Cyber Defence Competion is a team based competition were the team is dropped into a live environment that is being actively hacked.

  • NCL -- The National Cyber League is the first steps into learning cybersecurity, were the participates are guided through all topics in cybersecurity.
